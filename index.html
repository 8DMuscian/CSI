About CSI

The Computer Society of India (CSI) is the largest and most professionally managed association of and for IT professionals in India.

Our Mission

To facilitate research, knowledge sharing, learning, and career enhancement for all IT professionals, while simultaneously inspiring the next generation of computing talent.

What We Do

We organize workshops, seminars, conferences, and competitions to enhance technical skills and provide networking opportunities for students interested in computer science.

Our Community

Join a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts, developers, designers, and innovators who are passionate about technology and its applications.

# Why Join CSI?

  • Skill Development

    Access to workshops, training sessions, and hands-on projects to enhance your technical skills.

  • Networking Opportunities

    Connect with industry professionals, alumni, and like-minded peers.

  • Leadership Experience

    Opportunities to lead projects, organize events, and develop management skills.

  • Recognition & Certificates

    Earn certificates and recognition for your participation and achievements.
